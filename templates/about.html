About me

Welcome to my website! I am currently pursuing a Master's degree in Aeronautical Engineering. My academic journey has equipped me with a strong foundation in engineering principles, which I have applied to various projects, including the development of a cochlear implant and a delivery and sorting robot. These experiences have honed my technical skills and underscored the importance of teamwork and project management.

I am currently seeking internships in the aeronautical engineering field, particularly in automated systems. Eager to apply my knowledge and skills in a practical setting, I look forward to contributing to innovative projects and exploring potential opportunities for collaboration.
